Ground Beef Bolognese over Zucchini Noodles

⏱ 40 min🍽 6 servings✓ Strict Keto
440Calories
34gProtein
28gFat
7gNet Carbs

A proper Bolognese — ground beef slowly simmered with onion, celery, carrot (kept low), white wine, and whole milk, then reduced until the sauce is rich, dense, and deeply savory. Served over zucchini noodles with a blizzard of parmesan. The most satisfying ground beef format on the list.

Ingredients
·1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
·1 small yellow onion, finely diced
·2 stalks celery, finely diced
·1 small carrot, finely grated (keeps carbs low)
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1/3 cup dry white wine
·1/4 cup whole milk
·1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
·1/2 cup beef broth
·1 tsp dried oregano
·Pinch nutmeg
·Salt, black pepper
·Parmesan to serve
·4 large zucchini, spiralized
Instructions
1
Brown the Beef
Cook ground beef in a large Dutch oven over high heat, breaking apart. Let it brown without stirring 3–4 minutes. Drain excess fat. Remove beef.
2
Soften the Soffritto
In the same pot, cook onion, celery, and carrot in a little oil over medium-low for 8–10 minutes until completely softened and sweet. Add garlic and cook 1 minute.
3
Add Wine & Milk
Add white wine and cook until evaporated, about 3 minutes. Add milk and cook until absorbed, 2–3 minutes. This step — unusual but classic — tenderizes the meat.
4
Add Beef & Tomatoes
Return beef to the pot. Add crushed tomatoes, broth, oregano, and nutmeg. Stir well. Bring to a simmer.
5
Simmer Low & Slow
Cook uncovered over the lowest possible heat for 25–30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until sauce is thick and concentrated. Season generously with salt and pepper.
6
Serve
Quick-cook zucchini noodles in a hot pan 2–3 minutes. Plate with Bolognese and a generous mound of grated parmesan.

Notes

The milk step is authentic and transformative — it tenderizes the ground beef and creates a silkier sauce. Don't skip it.
The longer and lower you cook this, the better it gets. 25 minutes is minimum; 45 minutes is ideal.
This makes excellent leftovers and reheats beautifully. The sauce improves overnight.
One small grated carrot adds natural sweetness without significant carb impact — balanced by the other ingredients.
