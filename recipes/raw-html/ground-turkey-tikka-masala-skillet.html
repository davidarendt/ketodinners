Ground Turkey Tikka Masala Skillet

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
410Calories
34gProtein
26gFat
8gNet Carbs

All the warm, creamy, spiced comfort of chicken tikka masala — built faster and cheaper with ground turkey. A tomato-cream sauce loaded with garam masala, ginger, and garlic, spooned over cauliflower rice.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground turkey
·2 tbsp ghee or butter
·1 small yellow onion, finely diced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
·2 tbsp tikka masala spice blend
·1 tsp garam masala
·2 tbsp tomato paste
·1 cup crushed tomatoes
·3/4 cup heavy cream
·Salt to taste
·Fresh cilantro to garnish
·2 bags cauliflower rice, cooked
Instructions
1
Brown the Turkey
Melt ghee in a large skillet over medium-high. Add ground turkey and break apart as it cooks until no longer pink, about 6 minutes.
2
Build the Base
Add onion and cook 3 minutes until softened. Stir in garlic and ginger and cook 1 minute until fragrant.
3
Bloom the Spices
Add tikka masala blend, garam masala, and tomato paste. Stir constantly for 1 minute to toast the spices and caramelize the paste.
4
Simmer
Pour in crushed tomatoes and 1/4 cup water. Simmer 6–8 minutes until slightly thickened.
5
Finish with Cream
Reduce heat to low and stir in heavy cream. Warm through without boiling. Season with salt.
6
Serve
Spoon over cauliflower rice and shower with fresh cilantro.

Notes

Toasting the spice blend in the fat before adding liquid is the difference between flat and deeply aromatic — don't rush it.
Ground turkey keeps this leaner than the classic; add an extra tablespoon of ghee if you want it richer.
A squeeze of lemon at the end brightens the whole dish.
