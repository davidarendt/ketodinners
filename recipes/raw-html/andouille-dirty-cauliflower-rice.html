Andouille Dirty Cauliflower Rice

⏱ 25 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
390Calories
18gProtein
28gFat
8gNet Carbs

A keto take on Louisiana dirty rice — andouille sausage, celery, bell pepper, onion, garlic, and Cajun-seasoned cauliflower rice all cooked together in one cast iron pan until everything is deeply caramelized and smoky.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1 lb andouille sausage, diced small
·1 large head cauliflower, riced (or 2 bags frozen)
·2 stalks celery, finely diced
·1 green bell pepper, finely diced
·1 small yellow onion, finely diced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1.5 tsp Cajun seasoning
·1/2 tsp smoked paprika
·1/2 tsp dried thyme
·1/4 tsp cayenne (optional)
·Salt and black pepper
·2 scallions, sliced for garnish
Instructions
1
Brown the Andouille
Heat a large cast iron skillet over medium-high. Add andouille and cook 4–5 minutes until caramelized and fat rendered. Remove to a plate, leaving the fat in the pan.
2
Sauté the Trinity
Add olive oil to the rendered fat. Add celery, bell pepper, and onion — the Cajun 'holy trinity.' Cook 5–6 minutes until softened and starting to brown. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
3
Add Cauliflower Rice
Add riced cauliflower in a single layer. Do not stir for 2–3 minutes — let it brown and develop a slight crust. Then stir and cook another 3 minutes.
4
Season & Combine
Add Cajun seasoning, paprika, thyme, and cayenne. Season with salt and pepper. Return andouille to the pan. Toss everything together and cook 2 more minutes.
5
Serve
Taste and adjust seasoning — andouille is salty so go easy on added salt. Garnish with sliced scallions.

Notes

Letting the cauliflower sit undisturbed for the first few minutes creates the 'dirty' look and caramelized depth that makes this dish.
The Cajun trinity (celery, bell pepper, onion) is non-negotiable for authentic flavor.
Chicken livers (finely minced, cooked with the andouille) are traditional in dirty rice and add incredible richness for those who enjoy them.
This reheats beautifully — make extra for lunch the next day.
