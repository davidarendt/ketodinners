Ground Turkey & Poblano Skillet

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
390Calories
34gProtein
24gFat
8gNet Carbs

Mildly smoky roasted poblanos and ground turkey in a warm cumin-and-chili skillet, melted through with pepper jack and finished with avocado and cilantro. A weeknight taco bowl without the shell.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground turkey
·2 poblano peppers, diced
·1 yellow onion, diced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp ground cumin
·1 tsp chili powder
·1/2 tsp smoked paprika
·1/2 cup fire-roasted diced tomatoes
·1 cup shredded pepper jack
·1 avocado, sliced
·2 tbsp avocado oil
·Salt and fresh cilantro
Instructions
1
Soften the Poblanos
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add poblanos and onion and cook 5 minutes until softened and lightly charred.
2
Brown the Turkey
Add ground turkey and break apart. Cook until browned, 6–7 minutes. Stir in garlic and cook 1 minute.
3
Season
Add cumin, chili powder, paprika, salt, and the fire-roasted tomatoes. Simmer 3 minutes.
4
Melt the Cheese
Scatter pepper jack over the top. Cover 2 minutes until melted.
5
Serve
Top with sliced avocado and cilantro.

Notes

Poblanos are mild — for more heat, leave the ribs in or add a diced jalapeño.
Fire-roasted tomatoes add a smoky depth that regular canned tomatoes don't.
Spoon over cauliflower rice or into crisp romaine leaves.
