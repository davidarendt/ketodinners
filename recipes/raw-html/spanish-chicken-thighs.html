Spanish Chicken Thighs

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
430Calories
38gProtein
27gFat
6gNet Carbs

Chicken thighs braised in a bold Spanish-style sauce of smoked paprika, roasted peppers, capers, green olives, and a splash of dry sherry — the kind of dish that makes your kitchen smell like a tapas bar in Seville.

Ingredients
·4 bone-in skin-on chicken thighs
·1 jar (12 oz) roasted red peppers, drained and sliced
·1/2 cup green olives, sliced
·2 tbsp capers, rinsed
·1 small onion, diced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1/4 cup dry sherry (or dry white wine)
·1/2 cup chicken broth
·1 tsp smoked paprika
·1/2 tsp sweet paprika
·1/2 tsp dried thyme
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Sear the Chicken
Season thighs with salt, pepper, and sweet paprika. Heat oil in a wide oven-safe skillet over high. Sear skin-side down 5 minutes until deeply golden. Flip and sear 2 minutes. Remove.
2
Build the Sauce
Reduce heat to medium. Add onion and cook 4 minutes. Add garlic and smoked paprika, cook 30 seconds. Deglaze with sherry, scraping up all the fond.
3
Add the Rest
Add broth, roasted peppers, olives, capers, and thyme. Stir to combine. Nestle chicken skin-side up back into the sauce — the skin should stay above the liquid.
4
Finish
Simmer uncovered 15–18 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened. If the sauce looks thin, remove chicken and reduce 3–4 minutes. Serve over cauliflower rice.

Notes

Smoked paprika is the backbone — don't substitute with regular paprika.
Keeping the chicken skin above the sauce prevents it from getting soggy.
Sherry adds an authentic depth that wine can't quite replicate. A small bottle of dry (fino) sherry is worth keeping in the pantry.
