Chorizo & Roasted Pepper Egg Scramble

⏱ 15 min 🍽 4 servings ✓ Strict Keto
430Calories
28gProtein
34gFat
6gNet Carbs

Crumbled Mexican chorizo and smoky roasted peppers cooked down together, then eggs scrambled right into the pan — finished with sliced avocado and a squeeze of lime for brightness.

Ingredients
Scramble
·8 oz Mexican-style chorizo, casing removed
·6 large eggs, beaten
·1 jar (12 oz) roasted red peppers, drained and sliced (or 2 fresh red peppers, roasted)
·1/2 small yellow onion, diced
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tbsp olive oil
·Salt, pepper, and smoked paprika to taste
Toppings & Sides
·2 ripe avocados, sliced
·1 lime, cut into wedges
·Fresh cilantro (optional)
·4 cups mixed greens, lightly dressed
Instructions
1
Cook the Chorizo
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add chorizo and break apart. Cook 4–5 minutes until rendered and starting to crisp at the edges. Spoon off excess fat if desired (though the fat carries significant flavor).
2
Add Onion & Garlic
Add onion to the chorizo fat. Cook 3–4 minutes until softened. Add garlic and roasted peppers. Toss and cook 2 more minutes.
3
Scramble the Eggs
Beat eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper. Reduce heat to medium-low. Pour eggs into the pan over the chorizo and peppers. Gently fold with a spatula, cooking slowly until just set — slightly underdone is perfect as they continue cooking from the pan's heat.
4
Finish & Serve
Plate immediately. Top with sliced avocado, fresh cilantro if using, and lime wedges on the side. Serve with dressed mixed greens.

Notes

Mexican chorizo (raw, crumbled) is what you want here — not Spanish cured chorizo, which is a different product.
Low and slow is the key to scrambled eggs — high heat makes them rubbery. Pull them while still slightly underdone.
The fat rendered from the chorizo is deeply flavorful — use it to cook the onions rather than adding extra oil.
This is the fastest dinner on the entire 6-week plan. 15 minutes start to finish.
