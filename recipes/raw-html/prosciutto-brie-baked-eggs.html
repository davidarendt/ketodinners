Prosciutto & Brie Baked Eggs

⏱ 25 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
380Calories
24gProtein
30gFat
3gNet Carbs

Eggs baked over crisped prosciutto and peppery greens with molten pockets of brie and a swirl of cream. Feels indulgent and dinner-party worthy, comes together in 25 minutes.

Ingredients
·8 large eggs
·4 oz brie, sliced
·4 slices prosciutto
·1/2 cup heavy cream
·2 cups arugula
·2 tbsp butter
·Fresh chives to garnish
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Preheat
Heat the oven to 375°F.
2
Crisp the Prosciutto
Melt butter in an oven-safe skillet over medium. Lay in the prosciutto and cook 1–2 minutes until it starts to crisp.
3
Add Greens
Scatter the arugula over the prosciutto and let it wilt for 1 minute.
4
Add Eggs & Cream
Crack the eggs over the top. Drizzle the cream around them and tuck the brie slices between the eggs. Season with salt and pepper.
5
Bake
Bake 12–15 minutes until the whites are set but the yolks are still soft.
6
Serve
Garnish with chives and serve straight from the skillet.

Notes

Pull the pan while the yolks still jiggle — they keep cooking off the heat.
Brie melts into luscious pockets; a soft, ripe wedge works best.
Serve with a simple side salad — this is rich enough to stand alone.
