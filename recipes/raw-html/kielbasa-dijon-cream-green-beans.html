Kielbasa with Dijon Cream & Roasted Green Beans

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
490Calories
20gProtein
42gFat
8gNet Carbs

Browned kielbasa coins in a tangy two-mustard cream sauce, served alongside blistered roasted green beans. A rich, fast, deeply satisfying skillet dinner.

Ingredients
·1 lb kielbasa, sliced into coins
·1 lb green beans, trimmed
·2 tbsp olive oil
·3 tbsp butter
·3/4 cup heavy cream
·2 tbsp Dijon mustard
·1 tbsp whole-grain mustard
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·1/4 cup chicken broth
·Salt and black pepper
·Fresh parsley to garnish
Instructions
1
Roast the Green Beans
Heat the oven to 425°F. Toss green beans with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Roast 15–18 minutes until blistered and tender.
2
Brown the Kielbasa
Melt butter in a skillet over medium-high. Add kielbasa coins and brown on both sides, 5 minutes. Remove to a plate.
3
Make the Sauce
Add garlic to the pan and cook 30 seconds. Pour in chicken broth to deglaze, then stir in cream and both mustards. Simmer 3 minutes until thickened.
4
Combine
Return the kielbasa to the sauce and warm through.
5
Serve
Plate the kielbasa and sauce with the roasted green beans alongside. Garnish with parsley.

Notes

Two mustards give layered flavor — Dijon for sharpness, whole-grain for pops of texture.
Roast the green beans hot enough to blister; steamed beans go limp under the rich sauce.
Any smoked sausage works here if you can't find good kielbasa.
