Ground Beef-Stuffed Portobello Caps

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
400Calories
32gProtein
27gFat
7gNet Carbs

Meaty portobello caps roasted and piled with garlicky Italian beef and marinara, then blanketed in melted mozzarella. A knife-and-fork mini pizza with none of the crust.

Ingredients
·4 large portobello mushroom caps
·1 lb ground beef (80/20)
·1/2 cup low-sugar marinara
·1 cup shredded mozzarella
·1/4 cup grated parmesan
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Fresh basil to garnish
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Prep the Caps
Heat the oven to 400°F. Scrape the gills from the portobellos. Brush with olive oil, season, and roast gill-side up 8 minutes. Pour off any liquid.
2
Brown the Beef
Meanwhile, brown the ground beef in a skillet with the garlic and Italian seasoning. Drain excess fat. Stir in the marinara and parmesan.
3
Fill
Spoon the beef mixture into the roasted caps.
4
Top & Bake
Cover each with mozzarella. Return to the oven 6–8 minutes until melted and bubbling.
5
Serve
Tear fresh basil over the top and serve.

Notes

Scraping the gills and pre-roasting keeps the caps from flooding the filling with liquid.
Choose the largest, deepest caps you can find so they hold plenty of filling.
Add sliced pepperoni or a pinch of chili flakes for a pizza-supreme spin.
