Ground Chicken & Mushroom Cream Skillet

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
430Calories
36gProtein
30gFat
5gNet Carbs

Ground chicken and earthy mushrooms folded into a silky thyme-cream sauce with a hit of parmesan. Comfort food that comes together in one pan and lands over zoodles or cauliflower mash.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground chicken
·8 oz mushrooms, sliced
·1 shallot, minced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·3/4 cup heavy cream
·1/4 cup chicken broth
·2 tbsp butter
·1 tsp fresh thyme
·1/4 cup grated parmesan
·Salt and black pepper
·Fresh parsley to garnish
Instructions
1
Brown the Chicken
Melt 1 tbsp butter in a skillet over medium-high. Add ground chicken and break apart. Cook until browned, 6–7 minutes. Remove to a plate.
2
Sear the Mushrooms
Add remaining butter and the mushrooms. Cook undisturbed 3 minutes, then stir and cook 3 more until deeply golden.
3
Aromatics
Add shallot and garlic; cook 1 minute until fragrant.
4
Build the Sauce
Pour in chicken broth to deglaze, then add cream, thyme, and parmesan. Simmer 3 minutes until thickened.
5
Combine & Serve
Return the chicken, warm through, and season. Garnish with parsley and serve over zoodles or cauliflower mash.

Notes

Let the mushrooms sit undisturbed to develop color — moving them too soon makes them steam.
A splash of white wine in place of some broth makes this even more restaurant-like.
Parmesan both thickens and seasons the sauce, so salt last.
