Roasted Red Pepper & Goat Cheese Frittata

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
340Calories
20gProtein
27gFat
4gNet Carbs

Silky baked eggs studded with sweet roasted red peppers, wilted spinach, and tangy pockets of goat cheese. Elegant enough for company, easy enough for a Tuesday.

Ingredients
·8 large eggs
·1/2 cup heavy cream
·4 oz goat cheese, crumbled
·3/4 cup roasted red peppers, sliced
·2 cups fresh spinach
·1/4 cup red onion, thinly sliced
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Fresh basil to garnish
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Preheat
Heat the oven to 375°F.
2
Sauté the Base
Heat olive oil in a 10-inch oven-safe skillet over medium. Add red onion and cook 2 minutes, then add spinach and wilt, about 2 minutes.
3
Add Eggs
Whisk eggs with cream, salt, and pepper. Pour over the vegetables. Scatter the roasted red peppers evenly.
4
Dot with Goat Cheese
Drop crumbles of goat cheese across the surface.
5
Bake
Bake 15–18 minutes until the center is just set and no longer jiggly.
6
Finish
Cool 5 minutes, garnish with basil, and slice into wedges.

Notes

Pull the frittata when the center still has the faintest wobble — it firms up as it rests.
Jarred roasted red peppers are a great shortcut; pat them dry so they don't water down the eggs.
Goat cheese stays tangy in little pockets rather than melting away — don't over-stir it in.
