Andouille & Cabbage Skillet

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings ✓ Strict Keto
370Calories
18gProtein
28gFat
8gNet Carbs

Sliced andouille seared until caramelized, then braised with tender green cabbage, caraway seeds, whole grain Dijon, and a splash of apple cider vinegar. Humble, hearty, and incredibly keto-friendly.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1.5 lbs andouille sausage, sliced 1/2" thick
·1/2 head green cabbage, roughly chopped (about 6 cups)
·1 small yellow onion, sliced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1 tbsp whole grain Dijon mustard
·2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
·1/2 tsp caraway seeds
·Salt and black pepper
Simple Green Salad
·4 cups mixed greens
·Olive oil and white wine vinegar dressing
Instructions
1
Sear the Sausage
Heat a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high. Add andouille slices in a single layer. Sear 2–3 minutes per side until deeply browned. Remove to a plate.
2
Sauté Onion
Add olive oil to the pan. Add onion and cook 4–5 minutes until softened and slightly caramelized. Add garlic and caraway seeds, cook 30 seconds.
3
Add Cabbage
Add chopped cabbage to the pan. Toss to coat in the oil and drippings. Cook over medium-high 5–7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until cabbage is wilted and starting to char at the edges.
4
Season & Deglaze
Add Dijon and apple cider vinegar. Toss to combine. The vinegar will deglaze the pan and lift all the browned bits. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
5
Combine & Serve
Return andouille to the pan, tucking it into the cabbage. Cook together 2–3 more minutes to heat through and let the flavors marry. Serve with dressed greens.

Notes

Don't skip the sear on the andouille — the caramelized crust is what gives this dish its depth.
Cabbage is one of the most keto-friendly vegetables: low carb, high fiber, filling, and cheap.
Apple cider vinegar brightens the whole dish and cuts through the richness of the sausage fat.
Caraway seeds are the classic pairing with cabbage and andouille. They're subtle but noticeable — add them.
