Ground Chicken Piccata

⏱ 25 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
420Calories
38gProtein
27gFat
4gNet Carbs

The bright lemon-caper-butter magic of chicken piccata, built from tender seasoned chicken patties. A glossy pan sauce comes together in minutes right in the same skillet.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground chicken
·1 large egg
·1/4 cup grated parmesan
·2 tbsp almond flour
·3 tbsp butter
·1 tbsp olive oil
·1/2 cup chicken broth
·2 tbsp capers, drained
·1 lemon, juiced
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·Fresh parsley to garnish
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Mix the Patties
Combine ground chicken, egg, parmesan, almond flour, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
2
Sear
Heat olive oil and 1 tbsp butter in a skillet over medium-high. Sear patties 3–4 minutes per side until golden and cooked through. Remove to a plate.
3
Start the Sauce
Add garlic to the pan and cook 30 seconds. Pour in chicken broth and lemon juice, scraping up the browned bits.
4
Finish the Sauce
Stir in capers and the remaining 2 tbsp butter until glossy. Simmer 2 minutes to reduce slightly.
5
Serve
Return the patties to the sauce, spoon it over, and garnish with parsley.

Notes

Almond flour binds the patties without carbs and keeps them tender.
Don't skip scraping the browned bits — that fond is where the sauce gets its depth.
Serve over zoodles or sautéed spinach to soak up the lemon-butter sauce.
