Grilled Lemon-Herb Chicken Thighs

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings ✓ Strict Keto
390Calories
36gProtein
27gFat
4gNet Carbs

Bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs marinated in bright lemon, garlic, and oregano then grilled until the skin is shatteringly crispy and the meat stays juicy all the way to the bone.

Ingredients
Chicken & Marinade
·4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (~3 lbs total)
·3 tbsp olive oil
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1 lemon, zested and juiced
·1 tsp dried oregano
·1/2 tsp dried thyme
·1/2 tsp smoked paprika
·1 tsp salt
·1/2 tsp black pepper
Sides
·2 medium zucchini, sliced into 1/2" rounds
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and pepper
·4 cups mixed greens
·1 tbsp lemon juice + 2 tbsp olive oil for dressing
Instructions
1
Marinate
Whisk together olive oil, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, paprika, salt, and pepper. Coat chicken thighs thoroughly. Marinate at least 30 minutes at room temp or up to overnight in the fridge.
2
Preheat Grill
Heat grill to medium-high (about 400°F). Clean and oil the grates well.
3
Grill Skin-Side Down
Place thighs skin-side down. Do not move for 5–6 minutes. Let the skin render and release naturally — it will lift when it's ready. Once golden and crispy, flip.
4
Finish the Chicken
Cook another 10–12 minutes on the second side until internal temperature reaches 165°F. Rest 5 minutes before serving.
5
Grill Zucchini
Toss zucchini rounds in olive oil, salt, and pepper. Grill alongside the chicken 3–4 minutes per side until tender with grill marks.
6
Dress and Serve
Toss greens in lemon-olive oil dressing. Plate chicken with grilled zucchini and salad alongside.

Notes

Overnight marinade makes a noticeable difference — lemon tenderizes and penetrates deeper.
Resist moving the chicken for the first 5 minutes — letting the skin render completely prevents tearing.
Bone-in thighs are the best keto cut: higher fat content, more forgiving on the grill, impossible to dry out.
Leftovers are excellent cold over salad greens the next day.
