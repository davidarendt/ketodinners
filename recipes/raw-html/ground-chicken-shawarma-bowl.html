Ground Chicken Shawarma Bowl

⏱ 20 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
370Calories
36gProtein
20gFat
6gNet Carbs

Ground chicken seasoned with the full shawarma spice blend — cumin, turmeric, cinnamon, coriander, allspice, garlic — cooked until fragrant and caramelized. Served over cauliflower rice with cool tzatziki, pickled onion, and chopped parsley. All the flavor of shawarma in 20 minutes.

Ingredients
Shawarma Chicken
·1.25 lbs ground chicken
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1.5 tsp cumin
·1 tsp turmeric
·1/2 tsp cinnamon
·1/2 tsp coriander
·1/4 tsp allspice
·1/4 tsp cayenne
·1 tsp salt
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1 tbsp lemon juice
Bowl & Toppings
·2 bags cauliflower rice, cooked
·Tzatziki (Greek yogurt, cucumber, garlic, dill, lemon)
·Pickled red onion (red onion in red wine vinegar, sugar sub, salt)
·Chopped fresh parsley
·Tahini drizzle
·Cherry tomatoes, halved
Instructions
1
Season the Chicken
Mix all spices with salt. Add ground chicken and mix thoroughly — either in a bowl or directly in the pan. The spice blend should coat every bit of meat.
2
Cook High Heat
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add seasoned ground chicken, break apart. Cook 5–7 minutes until browned and cooked through, with some slightly crispy edges from the turmeric. Add garlic in the last minute of cooking.
3
Finish with Lemon
Remove from heat. Squeeze lemon juice over the chicken. Toss to combine. Taste and adjust seasoning.
4
Build the Bowl
Add cauliflower rice to bowls. Top with shawarma chicken. Spoon tzatziki alongside. Add pickled onion, chopped parsley, cherry tomatoes, and a drizzle of tahini.

Notes

Turmeric stains everything it touches — use an old pan or be prepared to clean promptly.
Quick pickled onion (red onion + red wine vinegar + sweetener + salt, 30 minutes) is worth making in advance — it keeps a week and elevates every Mediterranean bowl.
The spice blend here is the full shawarma profile. Don't be tempted to reduce the number of spices — the complexity is the point.
This is a great Tuesday-prep meal: mix the spices with the chicken and refrigerate overnight, then cook in 10 minutes.
