Crispy Tofu with Soy-Ginger Glaze

⏱ 25 min 🍽 4 servings ✓ Strict Keto
280Calories
18gProtein
20gFat
7gNet Carbs

Extra-firm tofu pressed until dry, pan-fried until each cube has a golden, crackly crust, then glazed in a sticky soy-ginger-sesame sauce alongside crisp stir-fried bok choy.

Ingredients
Tofu
·2 blocks (14 oz each) extra-firm tofu
·2 tbsp avocado oil
·2 tbsp cornstarch or arrowroot powder
·1/4 tsp salt
Soy-Ginger Glaze
·3 tbsp soy sauce (or coconut aminos)
·1 tbsp sesame oil
·1 tbsp rice vinegar
·2 tsp ginger, freshly grated
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp monk fruit sweetener or erythritol
·1/2 tsp chili flakes
Bok Choy & Serve
·4 heads baby bok choy, halved lengthwise
·2 cups cauliflower rice, cooked
·1 tbsp sesame seeds
·2 scallions, sliced
Instructions
1
Press the Tofu
Drain tofu and press firmly for at least 15 minutes (longer is better). Wrap in a clean towel, place a heavy pan on top. The drier the tofu, the crispier the result.
2
Cut & Coat
Cut pressed tofu into 1-inch cubes. Toss with cornstarch and salt until evenly coated.
3
Pan-Fry Until Crispy
Heat avocado oil in a large nonstick or cast iron skillet over medium-high until shimmering. Add tofu in a single layer — do not crowd. Cook 3–4 minutes undisturbed until golden on the bottom. Flip and cook all sides until crispy, about 8–10 minutes total.
4
Make the Glaze
While tofu cooks, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, ginger, garlic, sweetener, and chili flakes.
5
Glaze the Tofu
Pour glaze over crispy tofu in the pan. Toss over high heat 60–90 seconds until sauce reduces and coats each cube.
6
Stir-Fry Bok Choy
In a separate pan with a little oil, stir-fry bok choy cut-side down 2 minutes until browned. Add a splash of water, cover 1 minute to steam through. Season with salt.
7
Serve
Plate over cauliflower rice. Top with sesame seeds and scallions.

Notes

Pressing is non-negotiable. Even 30 minutes under a heavy pan makes a significant difference in crispiness.
Do not move the tofu for the first 3–4 minutes — it will naturally release when the crust has formed.
Cornstarch is the secret weapon for extra-crispy tofu. Arrowroot powder works as a keto alternative.
This is your one tofu meal for the full 6-week cycle — it's worth nailing the technique.
