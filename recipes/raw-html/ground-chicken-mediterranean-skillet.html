Ground Chicken Mediterranean Skillet

⏱ 20 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
370Calories
34gProtein
22gFat
7gNet Carbs

Ground chicken seared and tossed with cherry tomatoes, kalamata olives, artichoke hearts, lemon, and oregano — then finished with crumbled feta. A 20-minute weeknight Mediterranean trip that's genuinely delicious.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1.25 lbs ground chicken
·1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
·1/2 cup kalamata olives, halved
·1 can (14 oz) artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·3 tbsp olive oil
·1 lemon, zested and juiced
·1 tsp dried oregano
·1/2 tsp smoked paprika
·Salt and black pepper
·3 oz crumbled feta to finish
Serve Over
·2 bags (12 oz) cauliflower rice, cooked
·Fresh parsley, sliced cucumber
Instructions
1
Brown the Chicken
Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add ground chicken, break apart, and cook 5–6 minutes until browned and cooked through. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and paprika.
2
Add Garlic
Push chicken to one side. Add remaining tbsp oil and garlic. Cook 30 seconds, then mix into the chicken.
3
Add Tomatoes & Artichokes
Add cherry tomatoes and artichoke hearts. Toss and cook 3–4 minutes until tomatoes blister and release their juices.
4
Finish with Olives & Lemon
Add kalamata olives. Squeeze lemon juice over everything and add the zest. Toss to combine. Taste and adjust salt.
5
Serve
Plate over cauliflower rice. Crumble feta generously over the top. Garnish with fresh parsley and sliced cucumber alongside.

Notes

This is fast, light, and deeply flavorful — a weeknight Mediterranean dinner in 20 minutes flat.
Artichoke hearts from a can are perfectly fine here and save significant prep time.
The lemon juice added at the end is essential — it brightens and balances the olives and feta.
Ground chicken is leaner than beef or turkey — the olive oil and feta add the fat needed for keto macros.
