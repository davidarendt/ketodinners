Slow Cooker White Wine Chicken

⏱ 6–7 hrs🍽 6 servings✓ Strict Keto
430Calories
40gProtein
26gFat
5gNet Carbs

Bone-in chicken thighs braised low and slow in white wine, garlic, and mushrooms, finished with a swirl of cream into a glossy French-style sauce. Hands-off elegance from the crockpot.

Ingredients
·2.5 lbs bone-in skin-on chicken thighs
·1 cup dry white wine
·1/2 cup chicken broth
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 yellow onion, sliced
·8 oz mushrooms, quartered
·2 tbsp butter
·1 tsp fresh thyme
·1/2 cup heavy cream
·Salt and black pepper
·Fresh parsley to garnish
Instructions
1
Sear (Optional but Worth It)
Melt butter in a skillet and brown the seasoned thighs skin-side down 4 minutes. Transfer to the slow cooker.
2
Load
Add onion, mushrooms, garlic, thyme, white wine, and chicken broth around the chicken.
3
Cook
Cook on LOW 6–7 hours until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
4
Finish the Sauce
Remove the chicken. Skim fat, then stir the cream into the cooking liquid. For a thicker sauce, reduce it in a saucepan 5 minutes.
5
Serve
Spoon the sauce over the chicken and garnish with parsley.

Notes

Searing the skin first isn't required, but it adds color and depth the slow cooker can't.
Use a wine you'd drink — a dry Sauvignon Blanc or unoaked Chardonnay is ideal.
Serve over cauliflower mash to catch the sauce.
