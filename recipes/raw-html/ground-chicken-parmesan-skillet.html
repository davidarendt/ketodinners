Ground Chicken Parmesan Skillet

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
440Calories
40gProtein
28gFat
6gNet Carbs

Chicken parm reimagined as juicy seasoned patties simmered in marinara and blanketed with melted mozzarella — all the comfort, none of the breading. Broiled until bubbling and finished with fresh basil.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground chicken
·1 large egg
·1/4 cup grated parmesan
·1 tsp Italian seasoning
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1.5 cups low-sugar marinara
·1.5 cups shredded mozzarella
·Fresh basil to garnish
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Mix the Patties
Combine ground chicken, egg, parmesan, Italian seasoning, garlic, salt, and pepper. Form into 4 oval patties.
2
Sear
Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high. Sear patties 3 minutes per side until golden.
3
Add Sauce
Pour marinara around and over the patties. Simmer 5 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
4
Add Cheese
Scatter mozzarella over the top. Cover 2 minutes to melt, or run under the broiler until bubbling and spotty brown.
5
Finish
Tear fresh basil over the top and serve.

Notes

Mixing parmesan directly into the chicken seasons it all the way through.
An oven-safe skillet lets you go from stovetop to broiler in one pan.
Serve over zoodles or a simple arugula salad to catch the sauce.
