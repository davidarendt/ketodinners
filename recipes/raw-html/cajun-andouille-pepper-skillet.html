Cajun Andouille & Pepper Skillet

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings ✓ Strict Keto
390Calories
19gProtein
30gFat
8gNet Carbs

Smoky andouille sausage seared in a screaming-hot cast iron with sweet bell peppers, tender zucchini, and a hit of Cajun seasoning and lemon — bold, one-pan, done in 20.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1.5 lbs andouille sausage, sliced into 1/2" coins
·2 bell peppers (red and green), sliced
·2 medium zucchini, sliced into half-moons
·1 small yellow onion, sliced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1.5 tsp Cajun seasoning
·1/2 tsp smoked paprika
·1/2 lemon, juiced
·Salt and black pepper
Serve Over
·2 cups cauliflower rice, cooked per package directions
Instructions
1
Sear the Sausage
Heat a large cast iron skillet over high heat until smoking. Add 1 tbsp oil and andouille slices in a single layer. Sear 2 minutes per side until deeply browned. Remove to a plate.
2
Cook the Peppers & Onion
Add remaining oil to the pan. Add onion and bell peppers. Cook over high heat 4–5 minutes, stirring occasionally, until softened and slightly charred at the edges.
3
Add Zucchini & Garlic
Add zucchini and garlic. Toss and cook 3–4 minutes until zucchini is just tender. Don't overcook — it should still have bite.
4
Season & Combine
Sprinkle Cajun seasoning and smoked paprika over the vegetables. Return sausage to the pan. Toss everything together and cook 1–2 more minutes. Squeeze lemon over the top.
5
Serve
Serve over warm cauliflower rice.

Notes

A screaming-hot cast iron is the key — that char on the sausage and peppers is where all the flavor lives.
Andouille is already heavily seasoned — taste before adding extra salt.
Cajun seasoning brands vary widely in heat level. Start with 1 tsp and adjust to your heat preference.
Add a fried egg on top per serving to boost fat and make it even more filling.
