Baked Eggs with Spinach & Feta

⏱ 20 min 🍽 4 servings ✓ Strict Keto
300Calories
18gProtein
24gFat
4gNet Carbs

Eggs nestled into a garlicky sautéed spinach and cream base with crumbled feta, baked until the whites are just set and the yolks stay silky. Simple, elegant, deeply satisfying.

Ingredients
Baked Eggs
·8 large eggs
·5 oz baby spinach (about 5 packed cups)
·3 oz crumbled feta cheese
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·3 tbsp heavy cream
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1/4 tsp nutmeg
·Salt and black pepper
·Chili flakes (optional)
Sides
·2 ripe avocados, sliced
·Flaky sea salt for avocado
·Lemon wedges
Instructions
1
Preheat Broiler
Set broiler to high. Heat an oven-safe skillet over medium.
2
Sauté Spinach
Add olive oil and garlic to the pan. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant. Add spinach in handfuls, tossing until fully wilted — about 3 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
3
Add Cream & Feta
Pour in heavy cream and stir to combine with the spinach. Scatter feta evenly over the surface. Simmer 1–2 minutes until cream begins to reduce slightly.
4
Add Eggs
Make 8 small wells in the spinach mixture. Crack one egg into each well. Season egg tops with salt and pepper.
5
Broil
Transfer pan to broiler 5–6 inches from heat. Broil 3–4 minutes until whites are completely set but yolks are still runny. Watch closely — timing depends on your broiler.
6
Serve Immediately
Bring the pan straight to the table and serve with sliced avocado and lemon wedges alongside.

Notes

Cast iron or a well-seasoned oven-safe skillet is ideal — it goes from stovetop to broiler seamlessly.
The nutmeg in wilted spinach is a classical French combination — don't skip it, even just a pinch.
Broiler timing varies widely between ovens. Start checking at 3 minutes.
Serve immediately — eggs continue cooking in the hot pan, so the window for perfect runny yolks is short.
