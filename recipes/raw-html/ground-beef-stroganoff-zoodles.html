Ground Beef Stroganoff over Zucchini Noodles

⏱ 25 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
490Calories
36gProtein
34gFat
7gNet Carbs

Ground beef and mushrooms in a silky Dijon-cream-sour cream sauce with a splash of Worcestershire — all the comfort of classic stroganoff served over zucchini noodles instead of egg noodles. Rich, savory, and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients
Stroganoff
·1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
·10 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
·1 small yellow onion, diced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tbsp olive oil
·1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
·1 tbsp Dijon mustard
·1 cup beef broth
·3/4 cup heavy cream
·1/2 cup full-fat sour cream
·1 tsp paprika
·Salt and black pepper
·Fresh parsley or chives to garnish
Zucchini Noodles
·4 medium zucchini, spiralized
·1 tbsp olive oil
·Pinch of salt
Instructions
1
Brown the Beef
Heat a large skillet over high heat. Add ground beef and cook, breaking up, until well browned and slightly crispy in spots — about 6–8 minutes. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika. Remove to a plate.
2
Sauté Mushrooms & Onion
In the same pan, add olive oil. Add onion and cook 3 minutes. Add mushrooms and cook over high heat 5–6 minutes until deeply browned and all moisture has evaporated. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds.
3
Build the Sauce
Return beef to the pan. Add Worcestershire, Dijon, and beef broth. Scrape up any browned bits. Simmer 3–4 minutes until liquid reduces by about one-third.
4
Add Cream & Sour Cream
Reduce heat to medium-low. Stir in heavy cream and simmer 3 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in sour cream — don't boil after adding sour cream or it can curdle. Taste and adjust seasoning.
5
Cook Zucchini Noodles
Toss zucchini noodles in olive oil and a pinch of salt in a separate hot pan. Cook 2–3 minutes just until tender but still has bite. Don't overcook.
6
Serve
Plate zucchini noodles and spoon stroganoff sauce generously over the top. Garnish with fresh parsley or chives.

Notes

Browning the beef properly — not just cooking through but getting real color — is what separates good stroganoff from great stroganoff.
Add sour cream off heat. Boiling after adding it causes it to break and look curdled (tastes fine but looks unappetizing).
Cremini mushrooms work best here — they have more flavor than white button mushrooms and hold their shape.
This is ground beef at its absolute best — no cauliflower rice base needed, the zucchini noodles change the whole format.
