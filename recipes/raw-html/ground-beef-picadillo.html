Ground Beef Picadillo

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
420Calories
32gProtein
28gFat
8gNet Carbs

A savory-briny Cuban classic — ground beef simmered with tomato, green olives, and capers, warmly spiced with cumin and cinnamon. Keto-friendly and even better the next day.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground beef (80/20)
·1 yellow onion, diced
·1 green bell pepper, diced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1/2 cup crushed tomatoes
·1/3 cup green olives, sliced
·2 tbsp capers, drained
·1 tsp ground cumin
·1 tsp dried oregano
·1/2 tsp cinnamon
·1 bay leaf
·2 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Soften the Vegetables
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium. Add onion and bell pepper and cook 4–5 minutes until soft. Stir in garlic.
2
Brown the Beef
Add ground beef and break apart. Cook until browned, 7 minutes. Drain excess fat if needed.
3
Simmer
Add crushed tomatoes, olives, capers, cumin, oregano, cinnamon, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Add 1/4 cup water.
4
Reduce
Simmer 8–10 minutes until saucy and fragrant. Discard the bay leaf.
5
Serve
Spoon over cauliflower rice.

Notes

The olive-caper brine is the soul of picadillo — don't skimp.
Traditional picadillo uses raisins; we leave them out to keep it strict keto without losing the sweet-savory balance the cinnamon provides.
Makes fantastic leftovers and freezes well.
