Smoked Sausage Platter

⏱ 2 hrs🍽 6 servings✓ Strict Keto
520Calories
26gProtein
45gFat
6gNet Carbs

An easy, crowd-pleasing smoker day: an assortment of sausages kissed with smoke alongside charred cabbage wedges, served platter-style with mustard and pickles. Low effort, big payoff.

Ingredients
·2 lbs assorted smoked sausages (kielbasa, andouille, bratwurst)
·1 head green cabbage, cut into thick wedges
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1 tbsp BBQ rub
·Whole-grain and yellow mustard to serve
·Dill pickles to serve
·Salt and black pepper
·Wood chunks (hickory)
Instructions
1
Set Up the Smoker
Bring the smoker to 250°F with hickory.
2
Prep the Cabbage
Brush cabbage wedges with olive oil and season with BBQ rub, salt, and pepper.
3
Smoke the Cabbage
Place cabbage wedges on the smoker and smoke 1.5–2 hours until tender and smoky.
4
Smoke the Sausages
Add the sausages for the final 45–60 minutes, until heated through and the casings snap, 150–160°F internal.
5
Build the Platter
Slice the sausages on the bias and arrange with the cabbage wedges, mustards, and pickles.

Notes

Pick already-cooked smoked sausages so the smoke is about flavor, not food safety — you're just warming and bronzing them.
Smoked cabbage is an underrated keto side that soaks up all the sausage flavor.
A drizzle of the rendered sausage fat over the cabbage before serving is worth it.
