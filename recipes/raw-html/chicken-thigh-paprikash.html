Chicken Thigh Paprikash

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
450Calories
38gProtein
30gFat
6gNet Carbs

Hungary's most beloved chicken dish — thighs braised in a deeply paprika-scented onion sauce and finished with a generous swirl of sour cream. Rich, warming, completely unique in flavor profile, and comes together in 30 minutes.

Ingredients
·4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
·2 medium yellow onions, finely diced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp Hungarian sweet paprika
·1/2 tsp hot paprika or cayenne
·1/2 cup chicken broth
·1/2 cup full-fat sour cream
·2 tbsp butter
·1 tbsp olive oil
·1 tsp salt
·Fresh dill or parsley to garnish
Instructions
1
Brown the Chicken
Season thighs with salt. Heat oil over medium-high. Sear both sides until golden, 4–5 minutes per side. Remove to a plate.
2
Cook the Onions
Add butter to the pan. Add onions and cook 8–10 minutes over medium until deeply softened, golden, and almost melting. This is the flavor base — don't rush it.
3
Bloom the Paprika
Reduce heat to medium-low. Add garlic and both paprikas. Stir constantly for 30 seconds — paprika burns quickly on high heat. Add broth immediately.
4
Braise
Return chicken to the pan. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook 15 minutes until chicken is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
5
Finish with Sour Cream
Remove from heat. Stir in sour cream — always off heat to prevent curdling. Taste and adjust seasoning. Garnish with fresh dill. Serve over cauliflower rice.

Notes

Use proper Hungarian sweet paprika (Szeged brand is widely available) — it makes a dramatic difference over regular grocery store paprika.
Adding sour cream off heat is non-negotiable. Boiling it causes it to break into an unappetizing grainy sauce.
This is comfort food in its purest form — deeply savory, rich, and warming. Teddy will love it.
