Bacon & Mushroom Frittata

⏱ 20 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
350Calories
22gProtein
28gFat
4gNet Carbs

Crispy bacon lardons, deeply browned cremini mushrooms, caramelized onion, and melted Gruyère — all set in a creamy egg custard started on the stovetop and finished golden under the broiler. The most satisfying Friday egg dinner.

Ingredients
Frittata
·8 large eggs
·6 strips thick-cut bacon, cut into lardons
·8 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
·1 small yellow onion, thinly sliced
·2 cloves garlic, minced
·3 tbsp heavy cream
·3/4 cup Gruyère cheese, shredded
·1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
·1 tbsp olive oil
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Cook Bacon
Cook bacon lardons in a 10-inch oven-safe skillet over medium until crispy. Remove to a paper towel, leaving fat in the pan.
2
Caramelize Onion & Mushrooms
Add olive oil to the bacon fat. Add sliced onion and cook 5–6 minutes over medium until golden. Raise heat to medium-high, add mushrooms. Cook without stirring 4–5 minutes until deeply browned. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Season with salt, pepper, and thyme.
3
Mix Eggs
Whisk together eggs and heavy cream until smooth. Season with salt and pepper.
4
Add Eggs
Scatter bacon and 3/4 of the Gruyère over the vegetables. Pour egg mixture over evenly. Cook undisturbed over medium-low for 4–5 minutes until edges are set but center is still jiggly.
5
Broil to Finish
Top with remaining Gruyère. Transfer to broiler 4–6 inches from heat. Broil 2–3 minutes until top is golden and center is set. Watch closely.
6
Rest and Serve
Rest 2 minutes before slicing into wedges. Serve with simple dressed greens.

Notes

The key to great mushrooms is high heat and patience — don't stir for the first 4 minutes so they brown rather than steam.
Gruyère melts beautifully and has a nutty depth that elevates this beyond a simple frittata. Swiss works as a substitute.
Don't rush the broiler — the golden top is what makes this visually impressive and adds a slight caramelized crust.
Frittata is excellent cold or room temperature — great for a next-day lunch slice.
