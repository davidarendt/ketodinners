Ground Beef Moussaka-Style Skillet

⏱ 30 min🍽 6 servings✓ Strict Keto
430Calories
32gProtein
30gFat
9gNet Carbs

Everything you love about moussaka — cinnamon-spiced beef, tender eggplant, and a creamy ricotta top — collapsed into one weeknight skillet and finished under the broiler until golden.

Ingredients
·1.5 lbs ground beef (80/20)
·1 medium eggplant, diced
·1 yellow onion, diced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 cup crushed tomatoes
·1 tsp ground cinnamon
·1 tsp dried oregano
·3 tbsp olive oil
·1 cup whole-milk ricotta
·1 large egg
·1/2 cup grated parmesan
·1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
·Salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Brown the Eggplant
Heat 2 tbsp olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high. Add eggplant and cook 6–7 minutes until softened and golden. Remove and set aside.
2
Brown the Beef
Add remaining oil and the beef. Cook until browned, then add onion and cook 3 minutes. Stir in garlic.
3
Simmer
Add crushed tomatoes, cinnamon, oregano, salt, and pepper. Return the eggplant. Simmer 5 minutes.
4
Make the Topping
Whisk ricotta, egg, parmesan, and nutmeg together with a pinch of salt.
5
Broil
Dollop the ricotta mixture over the beef and spread gently. Broil 4–6 minutes until golden and set.
6
Rest & Serve
Rest 5 minutes before scooping.

Notes

Cinnamon in savory beef is the signature moussaka move — it reads as warmth, not sweetness.
Salting the diced eggplant for 10 minutes and patting dry keeps it from getting spongy.
The ricotta top stands in for the traditional béchamel and keeps it low-carb.
