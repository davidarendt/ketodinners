Pork Chops with Pepperoncini Pan Sauce

⏱ 30 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
480Calories
42gProtein
33gFat
4gNet Carbs

Seared bone-in pork chops in a tangy, buttery pepperoncini pan sauce — briny, garlicky, with just enough heat. A fast skillet dinner with big Italian-deli energy.

Ingredients
·4 bone-in pork chops (about 1" thick)
·2 tbsp olive oil
·2 tbsp butter
·1/2 cup sliced pepperoncini, plus 2 tbsp brine
·1/2 cup chicken broth
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1/4 cup heavy cream
·1 tsp dried oregano
·Salt and black pepper
·Fresh parsley to garnish
Instructions
1
Season & Sear
Pat the chops dry and season well. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high and sear 4 minutes per side until browned and 140°F internal. Remove to rest.
2
Start the Sauce
Lower the heat to medium. Add garlic and cook 30 seconds. Pour in chicken broth and pepperoncini brine, scraping up the browned bits.
3
Build
Add the sliced pepperoncini, oregano, and cream. Simmer 3 minutes until slightly thickened.
4
Finish
Swirl in the butter until glossy. Return the chops and any resting juices to the pan.
5
Serve
Spoon the sauce over the chops and garnish with parsley.

Notes

Pull the chops at 140°F — carryover heat brings them to a juicy 145°F as they rest.
The pepperoncini brine is the secret weapon; it makes the sauce bright without any added acid.
Serve over cauliflower mash or with a crisp green salad.
