Ground Turkey Greek Skillet

⏱ 25 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
380Calories
34gProtein
24gFat
7gNet Carbs

A leaner take on the Greek skillet — ground turkey with oregano and garlic, finished with wilted spinach, blistered tomatoes, kalamata olives, and plenty of crumbled feta over cauliflower rice.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground turkey
·2 tbsp olive oil
·3 cups fresh spinach
·1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
·1/2 cup kalamata olives, halved
·4 oz crumbled feta cheese
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·1 tsp dried oregano
·1/2 tsp cumin
·1 lemon, juiced
·Salt and black pepper
·1 bag (12 oz) frozen cauliflower rice
Instructions
1
Cook the Cauliflower Rice
Prepare cauliflower rice per package directions. Season with salt and a drizzle of olive oil; keep warm.
2
Brown the Turkey
Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add turkey and break apart, seasoning with oregano, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook until browned, 7–8 minutes.
3
Bloom the Garlic
Push turkey aside, add garlic to the empty space, and cook 30 seconds until fragrant. Stir together.
4
Add Tomatoes & Olives
Add cherry tomatoes and olives; cook 2–3 minutes until tomatoes begin to blister.
5
Wilt the Spinach
Add spinach in handfuls, stirring until wilted. Squeeze in the lemon juice.
6
Finish
Serve over cauliflower rice and top generously with crumbled feta.

Notes

Ground turkey can dry out — pull it off the heat the moment it's browned.
Full-fat feta in brine has far better flavor than pre-crumbled; crumble it yourself.
Great cold the next day as a lunch bowl.
