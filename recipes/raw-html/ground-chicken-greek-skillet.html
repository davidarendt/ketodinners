Ground Chicken Greek-Style Skillet

⏱ 20 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
360Calories
34gProtein
22gFat
6gNet Carbs

Ground chicken cooked with bright lemon, garlic, oregano, sun-dried tomatoes, and wilted spinach, then finished with crumbled feta. A lighter, brighter counterpart to the Greek beef skillet — same Mediterranean spirit, completely different feel.

Ingredients
Skillet
·1.25 lbs ground chicken
·3 cups fresh baby spinach
·1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes in oil, sliced
·4 cloves garlic, minced
·2 tbsp olive oil
·1 lemon, zested and juiced
·1 tsp dried oregano
·1/2 tsp dried basil
·Salt and black pepper
·3 oz crumbled feta
Serve With
·2 bags cauliflower rice, cooked
·Sliced cucumber
·Lemon wedges
Instructions
1
Brown the Chicken
Heat olive oil in a skillet over high. Add ground chicken, break apart, cook 5–6 minutes until golden and cooked through. Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil.
2
Add Garlic & Sun-Dried Tomatoes
Reduce heat to medium. Add garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Cook 1–2 minutes until garlic is fragrant and tomatoes have bloomed in the oil.
3
Wilt Spinach
Add spinach in handfuls, tossing until wilted — about 2 minutes. Squeeze lemon juice over everything and add lemon zest. Toss to combine.
4
Finish & Serve
Taste and adjust seasoning. Plate over cauliflower rice and crumble feta generously over the top. Serve with sliced cucumber and lemon wedges.

Notes

Sun-dried tomatoes in oil carry significantly more flavor than the dry-packed variety — use them if you can find them.
Ground chicken is lean, so the olive oil, sun-dried tomato oil, and feta provide the fat content needed for keto.
The lemon at the end is critical — it transforms this from a pleasant chicken dish to something genuinely bright and vibrant.
This is a great template: swap the Greek flavors for any Mediterranean profile and it works.
