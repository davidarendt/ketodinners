Ground Chicken Fajita Skillet

⏱ 25 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
380Calories
34gProtein
22gFat
7gNet Carbs

All the sizzle of chicken fajitas without the tortillas — ground chicken with charred peppers and onions, smoky fajita spices, and a squeeze of lime. Pile it over cauliflower rice or into lettuce cups.

Ingredients
·1.25 lbs ground chicken
·2 tbsp avocado oil
·1 red bell pepper, sliced
·1 green bell pepper, sliced
·1 yellow onion, sliced
·2 tbsp fajita seasoning
·1 lime, juiced
·Salt to taste
·1/2 cup shredded Mexican cheese
·1/4 cup sour cream to serve
·Fresh cilantro to garnish
Instructions
1
Char the Vegetables
Heat avocado oil in a large skillet over high. Add peppers and onion and cook, stirring occasionally, until charred at the edges, 5–6 minutes. Remove and set aside.
2
Brown the Chicken
In the same skillet, add ground chicken and break apart. Cook until browned, 6–7 minutes.
3
Season
Add fajita seasoning and 1/4 cup water. Stir and cook 2 minutes until the chicken is coated and glossy.
4
Combine
Return the peppers and onion to the skillet. Toss together and squeeze the lime over the top.
5
Serve
Top with shredded cheese, sour cream, and cilantro. Serve over cauliflower rice or in lettuce cups.

Notes

Cook the peppers hot and fast for real char — crowding the pan steams them instead.
Make your own fajita blend: chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, and a pinch of cayenne.
Ground chicken is milder than beef, so don't be shy with the seasoning and lime.
