Chicken Thigh Marsala

⏱ 25 min🍽 4 servings✓ Strict Keto
460Calories
38gProtein
28gFat
5gNet Carbs

Boneless chicken thighs seared golden and finished in a silky marsala-mushroom sauce with a touch of cream — the Italian-American restaurant classic made keto and even better at home.

Ingredients
·4 boneless skinless chicken thighs
·10 oz cremini mushrooms, sliced
·2 shallots, minced
·3 cloves garlic, minced
·1/2 cup dry Marsala wine (or dry white wine)
·1/2 cup chicken broth
·1/4 cup heavy cream
·2 tbsp butter
·1 tbsp olive oil
·Fresh thyme, salt and black pepper
Instructions
1
Sear the Chicken
Season thighs with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Sear 5–6 minutes per side until golden. Remove to a plate.
2
Brown the Mushrooms
Add 1 tbsp butter to the pan. Add mushrooms in a single layer. Cook undisturbed 4–5 minutes until deeply browned. Add shallots and garlic, cook 1 minute.
3
Deglaze with Marsala
Pour in Marsala — it will sizzle dramatically. Scrape up every brown bit. Add chicken broth and thyme. Simmer 3–4 minutes until reduced by half.
4
Finish the Sauce
Stir in cream and remaining butter. Return chicken and any resting juices to the pan. Simmer 3–4 minutes until sauce coats a spoon and chicken is cooked through (165°F).
5
Serve
Plate with a generous spoonful of sauce and mushrooms over each thigh. Pair with zucchini noodles or roasted asparagus.

Notes

Dry Marsala is essential — sweet Marsala makes this cloying. Find dry at any wine or Italian grocery store.
The fond scraped after deglazing is the backbone of the whole sauce — don't clean the pan before adding the wine.
This sauce also works beautifully with pork chops as a variation.
